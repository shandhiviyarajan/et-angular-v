FAQ

  • What Trades Are Available On Easy Tradies?
    The best way to find out if we supply temps for the profession you’re looking for is to type the name of the profession in the “profession” field on our find staff page.
  • What is Easy Tradies?
    Easy Tradies is a service that allows you to post vacancies you have for temporary staff. Once your job is posted and if their skill set matches what you’re looking for, temps can apply to work for you. Once hired temps can submit time sheets through the Easy Tradie website. You can likewise view and post reviews on employees, which help your future hire decisions.
  • How Do I Pay Temps I’ve Hired?
    Once your temp starts working for you, they’ll begin to submit timesheets through the Easy Tradies website for your approval. Once you approve the hours, they will be billed to your credit card automatically.
  • Is It Free To Register?
    Registering, posting jobs and hiring staff are all free to do. Easy Tradies will only charge you for hours on a temp’s timesheet that you have approved.
  • How Does Easy Tradies Work?
    The first step to using Easy Tradies is signing up for an account after your account has been approved you can post jobs to the Easy Tradies website.

    Once a job is posted our matching algorithm will contact temps that have the skills that you require and give them the option to apply. Our matching process will favour temps that have been shown to give employers a good experience.

    You can then select to hire the temps you want manually or you can have us automatically select the best ones for you based on their feedback scores.

    Once the hiring process is complete, the successful temps will get access to the full job details they need to arrive at your site prepared and on time.

    Each week the temp will submit a timesheet through the Easy Tradies website, once you approve their hours, your credit card will be billed for their time based on the pre-agreed hourly rate, or if you have a credit account we’ll email you an invoice.
  • Are There Any Fees For Using Easy Tradies?
    The temp’s hourly rate includes the Easy Tradies fee, which is a small percentage. It only gets billed once you have approved timesheets they have submitted.
  • How Long Will It Take To Get Responses To My Job Listings.
    The bigger centres will usually get responses from interested candidates within an hour.
  • Disputes Process
    The vast majority of jobs are completed without issues. In the rare case of a disagreement between an employer and a temp Easy Tradies suggests that you first, attempt to resolve the issue among yourselves. This is often the least complicated and fasted method.

 

Temporary Staff FAQ

  • Is Easy Tradies Free To Use?
    Yes – we do not charge our temporary staff to use the Easy Tradies platform.
  • What Trade Jobs Are Available On Easy Tradies?
    The best way to find out if we supply temps for the profession you’re looking for is to register as a temp.
  • What If I Only Want To Work In Certain Locations?
    Easy Tradies gives you the option to specify what locations you’d like to work in so you can choose to work in the areas close to where you live. It’s important to remember the fewer locations you select, the fewer job offers you’ll get.
  • About Ratings
    After every job, the employer will be given the option to review your work. A better review will make your profile more appealing to future employers, and helps you win more jobs. Easy Tradies will also send you more jobs the better your review score is.

    Some things you can do to ensure you get good reviews from employers are:

    • ● Only apply to jobs you’re confident you can complete to a high standard
    • ● Work efficiently and keep non-work related talk to a minimum
    • ● Arrive on time
    • ● Read the job description carefully, so you arrive prepared
  • Disputes Process
    The vast majority of jobs are completed without issues. In the rare case of a disagreement between an employer and a temp Easy Tradies suggests that you first attempt to resolve the issue among yourselves. This is often the least complicated and fasted method.
